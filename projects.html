CURRENT PROJECTS:

BONAIRE

Bonaire is a small and beautiful island in the Caribbean Sea. It has consistently been ranked as one of the finest snorkeling and scuba diving destination in the world. Also the island is blessed with ideal conditions for world-class windsurfing, kiteboarding, and other water sports and has become a very atractive place for tourism. This project consists in the development of a Marriott Courtyard with a contruction area of 40,000 mts approximately.

TURKS & CAICOS

Turks and Caicos is an archipelago (group of islands) with incredibly amazing beaches and vibrant coral reefs. It is located in the Caribbean Sea near Miami. . Although there are several hotels on the island and some of them you can consider luxury, the island lacks an international known hotel host. This project will be the first and its consists in the development of a Ritz Carlton in the area of Providenciales. This development is expected to boom tourism in the island and promote the growth of the economy and hospitality market.
