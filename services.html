BUY PLANNING

We analize your case in construction and/or remodeling; and study the blue prints, plots, and designs to find alternative products that adjust to your project's expectation.

During a project for a new hotel development, there are several phases regarding the construction process as well as the procurement process. Just as a product is assembled in a factory within a limited time, projects also have a deadline and require to complete a procedure to meet the objectives. The procurement of FF&E and OS&E including construction materials has to be planned in order to ensure the supply of materials for each work phase and to structure a budget and cashflow for the financial health of the project. for the financial health of the project.

PROCUREMENT

For the procurement we work directly with manufacturers and distributors that offer better prices and are more flexible to fulfill our requirements. We seek to give you an optimum cost and time for a low fee.

We will work professionally and ethically procuring the best valued products, safeguarding the client's interests and looking for the best development of the project. All of our transactions will be transparent to the client and we'll offer a real time visual status for all orders placed.
